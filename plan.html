Plan Your Trip

Everything you need to know for a smooth and memorable journey in Jharkhand.

Travel Essentials

How to Reach

Jharkhand is well-connected by air, rail, and road. Ranchi Airport and major railway stations make it accessible from all parts of India.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for sightseeing. Monsoon brings lush greenery, while summers are perfect for waterfalls and hill stations.

Suggested Itineraries

Choose from 2–3 day short trips covering Ranchi & Netarhat or 5–7 day itineraries including Betla, Deoghar, and adventure treks.

Travel Tips

Carry light woolens in winter, respect tribal culture, try local delicacies like Dhuska & Handia, and always carry cash for rural areas.

Where to Stay

From eco-resorts in Netarhat to modern hotels in Ranchi, Jharkhand offers budget-friendly as well as premium stays.

Getting Around

Buses, auto-rickshaws, and cabs are common in cities. For remote areas, hiring a private car is recommended.
